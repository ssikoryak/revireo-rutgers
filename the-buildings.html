Green Building Features

SUSTAINABLE SITE PLANNING

Avoided environmentally sensitive lands by redeveloping previously developed sites on campus

Channeled development into an area of the campus with existing infrastructure to support the new buildings

Protected and preserved other land on campus that provides habitats and contains natural resources

Designed to reduce pollution and land development impacts from automobile use by making the buildings sites as pedestrian friendly as possible

All toilets and faucets are ‘low-flow’ fixtures that minimize water usage, thus reducing the burden on the municipal water supply and wastewater systems

Water Efficiency

Each building is designed to consume at least 20% less water than a comparable building

The landscaped area is designed to minimize irrigation, and any irrigated areas use smart irrigation technology

Energy Efficiency

Enhanced building envelope and window performance

Optimized interior lighting and reduced exterior lighting

Use of ENERGY STAR equipment

High efficiency heating and cooling systems, and heat recovery systems

Use of refrigerants that minimize ozone depletion

All lighting and mechanical systems underwent inspections and calibrations to ensure optimal energy performance

INDOOR AIR QUALITY

More than 75% of the construction and demolition waste from the buildings was recycled, and thus diverted from disposal in landfills and incineration facilities

CONSERVATION OF MATERIAL
+ RESOURCES

Many recycled building materials and locally sourced materials were used in the buildings

Project team created an indoor air quality management plan, which includes such measures as scheduling, source control, and HVAC protection

All paints, coatings, flooring, sealants contain no volatile organic compounds

To further improve the occupants’ experience in the Honors College building, the project teams have provided a range of lighting and thermal controls for both individual spaces and multi-occupant spaces, such as classrooms and conference areas, to promote a productive environment
